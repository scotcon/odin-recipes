Beef Bowl Recipe

Description

Korean beef bowl over rice cooked with coconut aminos, soy sauce, minced garlic and gochujang.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Ground Beef
  • 0.5 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 0.5 Cup Coconut Aminos
  • 2 Mined Garlic Clove
  • 1 tbps Gochujang
  • 1 tbps Maple Syrup
  • 2 tbsp Spicy Mayo
  • 2 Green Onions
  • 3 Cup White Rice

Steps

  1. Combine Soy Sauce, Coconut Aminos, Garlic, Maple Syrup and Gochujang in a bowl or other container
  2. Brown ground beef
  3. Once browned, add in sauce mixture into the pan
  4. Put over rice and top with Green Onions and Spicy Mayo

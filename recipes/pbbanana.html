Peanut Butter Banana Toast

Description

Toasted sourdough topped with crunchy peanut butter and sliced bananas; a delicious treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 Slice Sourdough Bread
  • Crunchy Peanut Butter
  • 1 Banana

Steps

  1. Toast Sourdough Bread
  2. Generously coat toasted bread with crunchy peanut butter
  3. Slice banana into pieces and top over the crunchy peanut butter

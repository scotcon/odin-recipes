Scott's Egg Breakfast

Description

Scrambled eggs with toast topped with cottage cheese

Ingredients

  • 3 Large Eggs
  • Egg Whites
  • Sourdough Bread Slice
  • Low Fat Cottage Cheese
  • Pepper
  • Sriracha

Steps

  1. Mix 3 large eggs and egg whites in a bowl or other container
  2. Heat up pan and coat lightly with butter
  3. Pour egg mixture into pan and slowly, and continuously, mix the mixture in the pan until forms soft cloud-like consistency and slightly runny; remove from pan
  4. Toast sourdough slice
  5. Top egg mixture with sriracha
  6. Top toast with 1/2 cup of cottage cheese; grind pepper over the toast
